About

Who We Are

Blue Tusk Studio is an independent mobile game studio focused on crafting casual games with satisfying mechanics and polished feels.

We believe the best games are simple to understand, hard to put down — and always look good doing it. Every line of code, every animation curve, every tap should feel right.

Right now we're a one-person shop based in Vietnam, working out of pure curiosity and stubbornness.

What We Believe

A Few Hard Rules

Our compass for every design decision. Boring, but we mean them.

01

Feel over features

One mechanic done with great haptics, audio, and motion beats five half-baked systems. Always.

02

Respect the player

No dark patterns, no forced ads, no fake scarcity. If we want their money, we'll ask honestly.

03

Ship small, ship often

A small game that exists is worth more than a perfect one that never launches. We ship.

04

Build in public

Devlogs, screenshots, mistakes. The process is part of the story — we share it openly.

How We Work

From Spark to Ship

A loose three-phase rhythm. We jump back and forth, but the order rhymes.

Spark & Sketch

A new idea lives as a paper sketch for a week. If it still excites us, it earns a prototype.

Prototype & Playtest

Smallest possible playable build. Real hands on the screen as early as possible — feedback shapes the rest.

Polish & Ship

We don't say 'done' until the haptics, sound, and motion all land. Then we ship — and learn.
